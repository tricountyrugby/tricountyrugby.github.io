Special Events

The home game on September 17th is our annual Niagara Celtic Festival game! Join us in Olcott, NY for what promises to be an exciting matchup and great social afterwards. There will be food, drink, and activites around the park for friends and families. All players can bring a plus-one who will receive free admission to the festival.
