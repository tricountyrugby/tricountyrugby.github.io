Our goals as a club are to learn the game, play at our best, and above all have fun as a team. We welcome all new players, even those who have never touched a rugby ball before. We only ask that you come to practice ready to run and work hard.

Anyone interested in joining can contact us by email or on Facebook with any questions, or simply show up to a practice. We have practices at two different locations to accomodate team members who may have to drive farther to reach our home pitch in Lockport. Practices are currently held Mondays at the Pembroke pitch and Wednesdays at the Lockport pitch every week at 6:00pm.
